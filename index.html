About Me

Hello. I am Tim and welcome to my website! I am an engineer from South Korea. I studied Mechanical engineering and Interactive media in New York University Abu Dhabi. Studying engineering in my freshman year, I felt mechanical engineers do not consider athestetics and usability of the product as long as they are functional; the design team and engineering team often have conflicts due to their different viewpoints. This is excatly why I decided to study Interactive Media as well. I wanted to be an engineer who appreciates both athestetic and engineering aspects of the products. And the projects I work on incorporate elements of both engineering and art for this reason. You will see how fascinating and interactive engineering and art can be when they are put together!
