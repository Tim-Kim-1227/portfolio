My Skills

I am capable of designing products on computer softwares and manufacturing my own parts using devices like 3d printers, CNCs, and laser cutter. I have previous experience of making award winning robots since my middle school years and helping development of a mobile game and website in UI/UX design and databases.

 

My greatest skill is being able to design, prototype, manufacture, develop, and release a product without help from others. This gives me understanding of different processes involved in productions making. This is an impeccable skill as a modern engineer because effective communication with people from different fields is significant these days. In team-based projects, I will be capable of understanding requests from other departments and effectively implement them on the products.
